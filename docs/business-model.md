# Business Model

**Cash first, equity second.** The early business makes money from software
access and services before relying on long-term, illiquid equity outcomes. The
platform should be valuable before any startup exits — equity is upside, not the
proof that the operating business works.

## Revenue streams

| Stream | Pricing assumption | Why customers pay | Timing |
|--------|--------------------|-------------------|--------|
| **Investor subscription** | $500–$5K/mo (angels, syndicates, micro-VCs); $5K–$15K/mo (studios, corporate innovation) | Curated opportunities, thesis dashboards, sprint templates, operator network | MVP onward |
| **Sprint take rate** | 15–30% of sponsored sprint budgets; base 20% | Platform designs, manages, scores, and standardizes venture-building work | MVP onward |
| **Formation fee** | $5K–$25K when a validated opportunity becomes a company | Role design, incorporation workflow, cap-table planning, operating pack | After early traction |
| **Founder/operator placement** | $10K–$50K when an operator graduates into a founding role | Investors need tested people, not just available people | After marketplace liquidity |
| **Equity / carry upside** | Light: 0.5–3%; full studio model higher | Platform originated, validated, assembled, and de-risked the venture | Later, with legal rails |

Keep early equity light. Studio equity stakes benchmark around 20–40% (some
full-stack models 30–60%) because they contribute ideas, capital, teams, and
infrastructure — SignalFoundry should use small equity only when it truly
contributes to venture formation.

## Revenue scenarios (annual cash revenue)

Cash revenue excludes illiquid equity upside from companies created on the
platform.

| Scenario | Customer / sprint assumptions | Annual cash revenue | Interpretation |
|----------|-------------------------------|---------------------|----------------|
| **Conservative** | 100 investors @ $500/mo + 300 sprints/yr @ $6K (20% take) + 10 formations | ~$1.2M | A useful niche business; proves willingness to pay. |
| **Realistic strong** | 500 investors @ $1.5K/mo + 3,000 sprints/yr @ $8K (20% take) + 100 formations | ~$18.8M | Strong venture workflow marketplace with meaningful recurring revenue. |
| **Breakout** | 2,000 investors @ $2.5K/mo + 20,000 sprints/yr @ $10K (20% take) + 500 formations | ~$135M | Category infrastructure for early venture creation. |

> Most realistic 3–5 year target if the wedge works: **$10M–$30M annual cash
> revenue**, with separate long-term equity upside.

## First sales motion

Sell a limited investor membership ($1K–$3K/mo) for opportunity access plus the
right to sponsor validation sprints, then take 20% of sprint budgets. This makes
the platform revenue-positive **before** building a large two-sided marketplace.
