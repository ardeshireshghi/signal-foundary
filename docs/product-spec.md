# Product Spec

SignalFoundry turns a market thesis into a staged venture-building process. The
platform's agents do not replace investor judgment; they prepare the evidence,
break down the next work units, and make operator performance comparable.

## The five-stage venture loop

1. **Signal scan** — Detect market shifts, pains, regulation, funding activity, workflow changes, and underserved buyers.
2. **Thesis brief** — Create an investor-readable opportunity memo: buyer, pain, wedge, risks, comps, and why now.
3. **Sprint design** — Break the venture into small paid tasks with scope, rubric, timeline, and success criteria.
4. **Operator proof** — Operators complete sprints; AI and human reviewers score quality, judgment, and speed.
5. **Funding path** — Winning sprints unlock more budget, founding roles, company formation, and optional investment rails.

Each stage emits evidence into the [Evidence Graph](data-model.md).

## Worked example — AI compliance assistant for small dental clinics

An investor is interested in healthcare operations but does not want to write a
large check until customer pain, workflow fit, and operator quality are proven.

| Phase | Sprint | Budget | Evidence produced | Decision gate |
|-------|--------|--------|-------------------|---------------|
| 1 | Market map + competitor scan | $3K | Buyer segments, existing tools, regulation triggers, pricing hypotheses | Is the market worth interviews? |
| 2 | 30 customer interviews | $7K | Pain frequency, budget owner, urgency, quotes, workflow map | Is there real pull? |
| 3 | Landing page + waitlist test | $5K | Messaging tests, conversion rate, buyer objections | Can demand be captured? |
| 4 | Prototype + demo workflow | $15K | Clickable workflow, sample outputs, risk review | Can product value be shown? |
| 5 | Pilot design + founding role selection | $20K | Pilot LOIs, operating plan, founder/operator shortlist | Form the company or kill it |

> The investor does not buy an idea. The investor buys staged evidence and
> talent signal.

## Operator scoring

Operators are scored on the exact work required to build the venture, which is
more predictive than a resume:

- Research depth
- Customer empathy
- Synthesis quality
- Speed
- Commercial judgment
- Technical reasoning
- Ability to turn ambiguity into a next-step plan

Scores are attached to an **operator proof profile** that compounds across
sprints and feeds founder/operator placement.

## What we deliberately are not

Not a job board, not a crowdfunding portal, not a generic AI idea generator, and
not (in the MVP) a securities marketplace. See
[competitive positioning](#competitive-positioning) below.

## Competitive positioning

The differentiation is the combined workflow: **opportunity origination + paid
validation + operator proof + staged formation.**

| Category | Analogs | SignalFoundry difference |
|----------|---------|--------------------------|
| Venture studios | Antler-style / studio operators | More marketplace-like and investor-led; lighter equity; scalable sprint layer. |
| Fundraising infrastructure | AngelList SPVs, RUVs, rolling funds | Creates evidence and team signal *before* the funding vehicle. |
| Talent marketplaces | Toptal, Upwork, expert networks | Work is structured as venture validation and can graduate into founding roles. |
| AI research tools | Market research agents, databases | Reports become paid experiments, scored outputs, and formation decisions. |
| Accelerators | Cohort programs | Starts before a founder or company exists. |
